## 1. Who he is (High confidence)

- **Name:** António Pinheiro da Silva. Credited variously as **Tó Pinheiro da Silva**,
  **Tó Pinheiro**, **T. Pinheiro da Silva**, *Antonio Pinheiro Da Silva* (accent-stripped DB form).
- **Profession:** one of the most important Portuguese sound engineers — recording, mixing,
  mastering, restoration/remastering, production-adjacent studio work — plus musician and teacher.
- **Reach:** Portuguese popular music & **fado**, **alternative/indie**, **contemporary classical**,
  and **Lisbon electronic music** (the Príncipe label). This breadth is the key strategic finding.

## 2. Biographical claims — source-supported, NEED direct confirmation (Medium)

Mostly from a single Universidade Lusófona **event** bio (S01) and ETIC faculty page (S02).
Treat as leads to confirm with Tó / family / colleagues before publishing as definitive:

- Born in **Barreiro**; later studio base in **Oeiras**.
- Attended **Conservatório Nacional de Lisboa**; guitar + transverse flute; member of **Banda do Casaco**.
- **1982 — Abbey Road Studios training** (London).
- **Technical director, Valentim de Carvalho studios** (Paço de Arcos); reportedly the **first
  Portuguese engineer qualified on the SSL 4000E**.
- Developed **DELIRIUM** audio software.
- Teaching: **ECATI / Universidade Lusófona (ULHT)** and **ETIC**.
- Birth date: project infers **c. 1950** (70th birthday gathering, Oct 2020 — see S26). **Confirm exact date.**
